My name is Eden. I started taking photos in 2021. It began as a form of self-expression - developing later into a means of giving thanks. My identity as a trans woman developed independently alongside my identity as a photographer. The queer people, specifically the trans people in my life, helped shape who I am now. Taking photos and showing the beautiful souls that I see on my end of the camera is my way of giving thanks to them for being the authentic people that they are. I specialize in displaying my subjects the way they want to be seen - mainly through portraits. I prefer to shoot in every individual's natural environment: be it their studio, their home, wherever they feel most comfortable. Color and black and white options are available. I shoot in 6x7 medium format. Sliding scale pricing.
